## Pre-Race Packing Order

1. Buy all supplies from this list
2. Pack the 11 individual drop bags (per `drop-bags.md`)
3. Label each bag clearly — name + mile
4. Load crew vehicle bins (nutrition, clothing, foot care, medical, sleep)
5. Confirm crew vehicle fits race specs (max 20 ft × 8.5 ft)
6. Submit bags at check-in per timeline above
7. Keep crew bins in vehicle for restocking at each stop
