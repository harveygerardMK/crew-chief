Pacers are allowed starting at **Loon Lake (mile 87.6)** and at **Crew Access aid stations** after that. Pacers may NOT carry Harvey's food, water, or gear (automatic DQ if they do, except in a medical emergency — which also DQs).

**Legal pacer pickup / handoff points (the ONLY places a pacer can start or finish a leg):**
Loon Lake (87.6), Tahoe City (130.0), Brockway Summit (148.6), Village Green (163.6), Finish (200.4).

**NOT pacer swap points** — a pacer may run *through* these but cannot start or finish a leg here:
Barker Pass (103.1), Stephen Jones Memorial (110.6), Spooner Summit (182.3).

This is why there are exactly **four** pacer legs, not six.
