## The course

2026 is the full loop: 200.4 miles around Lake Tahoe, roughly 64,000 feet of up and down combined (gain plus loss).

## The loop, in plain terms

We start at Heavenly Stagecoach Lodge on the south shore (Nevada), head west and south through Sierra-at-Tahoe country, then northwest toward Wrights Lake and Loon Lake in the Desolation fringe. From Loon Lake we climb east over Barker Pass on the west shore, run north along the rim through Tahoe City and up to Brockway Summit, cross to the east side at Village Green (Incline area), then drop south on the Nevada side to Spooner Summit before climbing back to Heavenly.

## Aids and pacing

Thirteen aid stations, segments from 7.5 to 19.4 miles. Three sleep stations: Wrights Lake (mile 70.7), Barker Pass (103.1), Brockway Summit (148.6).

Crew can hit seven stops: start/finish (Heavenly), Sierra at Tahoe, Loon Lake, Tahoe City, Brockway Summit, Village Green, and the finish. Pacers start at Loon Lake (mile 87.6).

## What I'm expecting on trail

- Tahoe Rim Trail is well marked; the race adds reflective ribbons ("dragons") and arrows at junctions. Still carry GPX — wind and wildlife mess with signs.
- Long backcountry stretches with little or no cell service.
- Water can be 20+ miles apart and isn't guaranteed. I'm carrying 3 liters capacity.
- Yellow-legged frog habitat crosses the route — stay on trail, single file.
